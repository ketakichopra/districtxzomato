Where This Starts

From Zomato,
District has evolved.

A decade of serving the UAE's restaurants showed us where the old model fell short for premium operators: discount-led, delivery-led, built for everyone. So we listened, found the gaps, and built a separate app from the ground up.

District is the one-stop app for everything going out — dining, events, concerts, attractions and activities — where each vertical feeds the next. For an integrated resort, that is not a feature. It is the whole point.
